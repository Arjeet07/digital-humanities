Dr. Saleem Mir

Dr. Saleem Mir is a loving, charming, and cool professor who is teaching Digital Humanities and Heritage Tourism at the Cluster Innovation Centre, University of Delhi. He is a native of Kashmir and has studied Bachelors and Masters in Geography at the Delhi School of Economics and Aligarh University. He completed his Ph.D. in the Geography of Tourism from the Delhi School of Economics.In addition to his expertise in the field of Geography and Tourism, Dr. Mir has a keen interest in digital humanities and heritage tourism. He uses his knowledge to create innovative and engaging coursework that helps students develop a deeper understanding of these subjects. He encourages his students to think critically and creatively, challenging them to find new solutions to real-world problems.
