Voyant Tools is a web-based text analysis and visualization tool that allows users to upload and analyze their own texts or analyze publicly available texts. It is designed for scholars, students, and anyone who wants to explore the patterns and trends in large bodies of text. With Voyant Tools, users can perform a variety of text analysis tasks, such as word frequency analysis, keyword in context (KWIC) analysis, and sentiment analysis. The tool also provides visualizations of the text, such as word clouds, word frequency charts, and topic models, to help users explore the patterns and trends in the data. Voyant Tools is easy to use and does not require any specialized technical knowledge. Users simply need to upload their text or enter a URL, and the tool takes care of the rest. The results are displayed in an intuitive interface that allows users to interact with the data and explore it in different ways. Overall, Voyant Tools is a powerful and user-friendly tool that can help users gain insights into large bodies of text and discover patterns and trends that might otherwise be difficult to uncover.
